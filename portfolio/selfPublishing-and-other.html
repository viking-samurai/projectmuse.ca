Guild Saga

  • Take a look.

Guild Saga is an NFT-based, crypto-mining game, by independant game developer: Occelot Technologies. They needed a website that would coincide with the launch of several new updates and features to their core game, as well as an expansion game. It is the only time in my career so far that I've worked with a dedicated designer, and I'm proud of the results!

This web application was a legacy project that I took over from a developer about to go on a paternity leave. As such, it's not written in one specific style, but actually two styles that are blended together. I wrote most of the JavaScript in the site, but other programming languages feature prominently, and I had little to do with the coding in those languages.

Guerilla Teacher, vol. 1

  • Take a look.

Guerrilla Teacher, vol 1 is actually my Master's thesis from the University of Calgary. I was going through a lot of change at the time, as I had just moved out here, and it was a way to keep calm during a rough situation. It's also an interesting look at how to present massive amounts of text in website form, a feat that isn't normally seen, and seldom done effectively. I'm proud of how it turned out.

The website is written in HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, with significant amounts of custom coding coding to create its unique navigational experience. The website could easily be converted into a book, brochure, manual, or any other medium designed to hold significant amounts text or photos. It can also be attached to a database for a smoother experience, with much faster loading speeds.
