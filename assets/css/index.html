Collaborative Learning

Collaborative learning is a new look at group learning that promotes both teamwork and individual growth, for students looking to learn a similar goal at a similar pace. At E-mmersion, all of my courses are offered as either one on one instruction, or as collaborative learning opportunities, with up to four students per class.
